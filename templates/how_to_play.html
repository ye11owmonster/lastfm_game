HOW TO PLAY
LAST.FM.
game
  1. You need to have a Last.fm account with at least 100+ scrobbled artists. Otherwise the game won't be interesting.
  2. On the Home page enter your Last.fm username and click on "Get random artists" button.
  3. List of 5 artists will be generated for you, with links to YouTube and Last.fm. Pick any (or several!) and start exploring.
    Watching music videos that you've never seen before is highly recommended!
  4. Try entering other usernames! Last.fm scrobbling history is open for everyone.
